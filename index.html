About

Hello !! I am Tribikram Dhar, a research engineer at TCS Research and Innovation Labs. I pursued my B.E in Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University,Kolkata, India.

I am currently a part of the Smart Machines and Automation group in TCS Research and Innovation Labs based in Bengaluru (Bangalore), Karnataka, India. I work on robotics hardware kinematics and deep reinforcement learning. I have previously worked on applications of deep learning in medical imaging.

Presently, I am not open to collaboration but if you have any enquiries about by work or about TCS Research you can reach me out at at tribikram.d@tcs.com.
